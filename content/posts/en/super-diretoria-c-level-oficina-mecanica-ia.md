## The 5 Virtual Directors in Your Shop

### 1. 🧠 CEO Agent (Chief Executive Officer)
* **Role:** Board President.
* **Function:** Consolidates reports from all directors, strips out noise, and delivers an executive report featuring **Top 3 Daily Priority Actions** and a **Brutally Honest Analysis** of your operations.

### 2. 💰 CFO Agent (Chief Financial Officer)
* **Role:** Financial Director.
* **Function:** Audits 7-day revenue, breaks down average ticket (Parts vs Labor), and pinpoints exact money stuck in unapproved estimates.

### 3. 📢 CMO Agent (Chief Marketing Officer)
* **Role:** Sales & Marketing Director.
* **Function:** Scans chat history and rescues "Limbo Leads" (customers who received quotes on WhatsApp over the last 90 days but didn't close), generating persuasive copy ready for sending.

### 4. 📋 PM Agent (Product & Operations Manager)
* **Role:** Shop Floor Operations Manager.
* **Function:** Monitors lift and bay occupancy. If a technician accumulates 6+ active orders, issues an overload alert to redistribute jobs and avoid delays.

### 5. ⚙️ CTO Agent (Chief Technology Officer)
* **Role:** Technology Director.
* **Function:** Tracks the stability of n8n automations, WhatsApp API connections, and notifications to ensure zero lost messages.
