There is a golden rule in automotive sales: **The customer does not approve what they do not understand.**

When you send a PDF on WhatsApp with a giant list of technical terms ("Replacement of sway bar link, CV joint, and control arm bushing"), the average customer gets scared of the price and asks for time to think. While they "think," their car keeps occupying your lift. You lose money twice, and we have already shown [how to identify and avoid idle cars](/post/identificar-carros-parados-dinheiro-perdido-oficina).

## Translating "Mechanic Jargon" with Artificial Intelligence

The solution adopted by **CoreAutoCRM** to increase approvals and clear the shop floor fast is to put Artificial Intelligence in the middle of the communication process (in what we call Routing Modules).

Instead of sending the raw list, the advisor asks the AI to formulate the message. The AI steps in and "translates" the technical part into the actual safety benefit:

*"Hi Mark, we inspected the Civic. That suspension part (sway bar link) that keeps the car stable in curves broke. For safety, especially if you drive on the highway, we need to replace it. The total value with labor is $X."*

The customer understands the value of safety, feels respected, and approves faster. Everything directly within a fluid conversational interface on WhatsApp, without requiring file downloads or long phone calls.

Use AI to communicate better (take the opportunity to also learn [how to do smart follow-up](/post/como-fazer-follow-up-inteligente-oficina)), and your estimate approval rate will skyrocket while the idle time of vehicles in your shop plummets.
