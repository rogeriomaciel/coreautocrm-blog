There is a question every auto repair shop owner or receptionist hears at least 30 times a day: **"Hey boss! Is my car ready yet?"**.

This innocent question is one of the biggest productivity killers in an automotive service center. Every time the phone rings or a WhatsApp message arrives asking about vehicle status, the service advisor (or the mechanic) has to pause work, walk out to the bay, check the repair phase, and reply.

The good news is you can eliminate 80% of these interruptions by implementing **Automated Repair Order (RO) Notifications** via WhatsApp.

## How does Automatic Notification work in practice?

The secret to automatic notification is connecting shop floor management to customer communication in real time. When a mechanic changes a vehicle's stage in the shop workflow, the system automatically sends a crisp, professional message to the vehicle owner's WhatsApp.

### The Automated Status Workflow:

1. **Vehicle Received:** *"Hi Mark, your Ford Focus has arrived at the shop. Click here to view your digital Inspection Checklist with photos."*
2. **In Diagnosis / Quoting:** *"Mark, the diagnostic check on your vehicle is complete. Click the link to review the required parts and approve the estimate."*
3. **In Maintenance:** *"Estimate approved! Your vehicle is now on the lift being worked on by our technicians."*
4. **Ready for Pickup:** *"Great news! Your vehicle is fully serviced and detailed. You can pick it up today by 6:00 PM."*

## 3 Immediate Benefits for Your Shop

### 1. Eliminates Customer Anxiety
Customers call repeatedly because they feel anxious and lack visibility. When they receive proactive mobile updates at every milestone, they feel reassured and stop calling every 30 minutes.

### 2. Boosts Team Productivity
If your reception team spends 2 hours a day answering status calls, those 2 hours can now be spent closing new repair jobs, sourcing parts at better rates, or providing premium in-person service.

### 3. Elevates Brand Perception & Professionalism
A shop that sends clean, automated WhatsApp updates projects the image of a luxury dealership. This justifies premium labor rates and drives word-of-mouth referrals.

## How to implement with CoreAutoCRM?

In **CoreAutoCRM**, this workflow is native. Mechanics don't need to type lengthy text messages or navigate clunky software.

Using a simple Kanban-style dashboard or mobile app on the shop floor, moving a car card from "In Repair" to "Ready" triggers Artificial Intelligence to generate and send an instant WhatsApp notification to the customer, complete with final invoice totals and instant payment links.

Let technology handle routine updates so your team can focus on delivering flawless repairs and increasing profitability.
