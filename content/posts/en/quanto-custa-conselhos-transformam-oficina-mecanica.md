To answer this question honestly, we must analyze cost from two perspectives: **direct financial cost** and **the invisible cost of inaction (the money you lose every day without realizing it)**.

---

## 1. The Traditional Cost: Hiring a C-Level Executive Board

If a medium auto shop wanted to hire human executives to perform the roles that the [AI Board](/post/super-diretoria-c-level-oficina-mecanica-ia) executes autonomously, the math would be impossible:

* **CFO (Chief Financial Officer):** ~ $8,000 / month
* **CMO (Sales & Marketing Director):** ~ $7,000 / month
* **PM (Operations Manager):** ~ $5,000 / month
* **Governance Consultant:** ~ $6,000 / project
* **Estimated Total:** **Over $20,000.00 per month in payroll.**

For 99% of auto repair shops, this expense is out of reach.

## 2. The Invisible Cost: How much are you losing TODAY without AI?

Most owners focus solely on software pricing while ignoring the financial black hole caused by lack of shop floor governance:

### A. Forgotten Estimates in WhatsApp Limbo
In a typical shop with 3 lifts, about 15 to 20 estimates expire per month on WhatsApp without follow-up due to lack of front desk time.
* If just 5 of those estimates average $300 each, your shop is **leaving $1,500 on the table every month**.

### B. Cars Idle on Lifts Awaiting Approvals
A lift blocked for 2 days waiting for customer approval or technician reassignment represents an immense operational productivity loss.

### C. Owner Time Wasted Putting Out Fires
When the owner spends 3 hours a day manually typing data into rigid ERPs, they stop focusing on strategic business growth.
